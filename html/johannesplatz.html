he Krampus

The Lienz Krampus Days on December 3, 5, and 6 are a thrilling highlight of the Advent season.

Following an ancient tradition to chase away the cold, young men don hand-carved wooden masks, thick furry costumes, and large bells to become "Krampuses" for parades and processions.

The Krampus parades take place at different locations, one of them the downtown of Lienz. Barriers and stewards ensure safety while maintaining the lively atmosphere.
