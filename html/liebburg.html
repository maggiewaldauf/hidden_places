T

he Liebburg Adventcalendar

A highlight of Christmas in Lienz is the Large Art Calendar displayed on the 24 windows of the Liebburg, Lienz's town hall. For the 27th year, artists transform these windows into unique calendar images.

Each evening at 18:30, the market becomes a cultural gathering as visitors watch the unveiling of a new window, creating a festive atmosphere.

The original calendar artworks will be auctioned in February 2025. Organized by Round Table 22 Lienz with city support, the auction proceeds benefit local individuals in need and the charity Licht ins Dunkel.

N

ativity Scene Exhibition

In the Liebburg’s ground floor, the Lienz Nativity Scene Association showcases handmade nativity scenes.

It is a well-known tradition for Austrian families to craft their own crib, in the Oriental or Tyrolean style of a stable. The crib is typically set up and decorated on Christmas eve.

The origin of the tradition lies in the strong bond of Austria and catholicism. The Christmas story of Maria and Joseph, searching for a place to rest, is still a very popular scene for theater plays, in churches as well as in schools.

The exhibit invites visitors to admire the artistry and revisit childhood memories.

Opening hours:

6 Dec – 15 Dec

Entry: Free
