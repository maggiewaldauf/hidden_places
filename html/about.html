ienz at Christmas Time

In winter, Lienz typically experiences cold weather with average temperatures ranging from -5°C to 5°C (23°F to 41°F). Snowfall is common, yet the weather is generally stable.
Visitors around Christmas should prepare for chilly conditions and bring warm clothing, especially for outdoor activities and evening events.
It is important to check the forecast regularly and to get informed about possible avalanche warnings.

M

odes of Transportation

Lienz is easily accessible by various modes of transport. The closest airport is Innsbruck Airport, about 2.5 hours by car, bus or train. Alternatively, Vienna International Airport is around 4.5 hours away by car or train.
The Town’s compact size makes it easy to explore on foot. For destinations that lie a bit further, it is the quickest to take the bus or train, from the central bus and train station.
